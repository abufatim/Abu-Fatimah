Scrambled egg

Eggs are one of my favourite foods. Here is one delicious egg that can be prepared for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat
  2. Gently mix the egg and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add egg and cream
  4. Use spatula to fold the eggs for every 20 seconds and fry over low heat
  5. When eggs are still moist remove from heat and serve
